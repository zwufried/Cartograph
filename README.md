I'm an avid reader of fantasy novels, and one thing most of them have in common is a map illustration at the beginning. Many of these maps have no scale, and there have been many times where characters will travel from a location on one side of the continent to another on the other end within a matter of days. I can't help but wonder: does the story take place on a small island or continent? Is the entire planet the size of Pluto? Are horses faster in this world than in ours? I built Cartograph using Claude because it allows the user to answer these questions for their fictional world. Hope you have fun with it!
